Here is my Curriculum Vitae.

I am a graduate student at Shantou University (STU) and my advisor is Jianlong Xu . I receive my bachelor degree in Jiangxi University of Science and Technology in 2019. I am working at Hisun Technology from April 2019 to August 2020.

I like programming and discussing some interesting computer technologies .

Research

I work on federated learning for service recommendation, focusing on the study of federated learning convergence problems in heterogeneous settings.
